We’re chipping into VISA!

 

To further support your financial needs into the future, we will convert our debit card program from our current Mastercard™ program to a new and improved VISA® chip-enabled debit card program effective April 29, 2019.

 

This card program upgrade is an important advancement for the credit union and for you as it promises enhanced security and wider acceptance. Upgrading to a VISA® debit card program provides you with chip enhancement which adds card protection for you and enables you to use your card more easily worldwide.

 

The new VISA® chip-enabled debit cards will be automatically mailed out to all members with an existing Mastercard debit card on April 15, 2019. Please allow 7 – 10 business days to receive your card. If you have not received a card by April 26, 2019, please call 478-745-0494.

 

To ensure your card arrives at the right mailbox and in a timely manner, please ensure your contact information is up to date by calling 478-745-0494 or updating your information in your online banking.

 

Please know our leadership and staff is committed to making this card change a smooth and easy process for all. To help you better understand the conversion and how it affects you, we have created a Member Resource Guide for your review.

 

In addition, our staff will be on hand to help walk you through any questions or concerns you have. We hope you will take the time to review this information to fully understand the coming changes and how they will affect you. We thank you in advance for your patience throughout the process and, as always, for your loyal membership.
