We’re chipping into VISA!

 

Congratulations! Your enhanced CGR Credit Union VISA® debit card with chip technology is on the way and broader worldwide acceptance when used at a chip reader. This VISA® card will replace the Mastercard debit card you’ve been using.

 

Your card should arrive within 7-10 business days. If you do not receive a debit card by April 26, 2019, please call 478-745-0494 to notify us.

 

What's changing:

• Your account number
• Your card’s expiration date and security code (three digits on the back of the card)
• Your PIN
• Telephone numbers on the back of your card
• Your new card has an embedded chip and signature feature for added security and wider acceptance outside the U.S.

 

What’s not changing:

• Our commitment to you – our card specialists are here to help you when you need us.

 

What you need to do when you receive your new card:

• Activate your new card by calling the number on the sticker attached to the front of your new card.
• Destroy your old Mastercard debit card. All Mastercard activity will be shut off effective April 29, 2019. You will need to destroy your Mastercard and activate your new VISA® card by this date.
• Update merchants who automatically bill you and/or with whom you have your debit card on file for charges (Utilities, Cable provider, Amazon, Netflix, etc).

You can access additional information online at www.cgrcu.org/DebitCard2019.pdf. If you have any questions, please call us. We are here for you and want to help make this conversion as pain-free as possible.

 

Thank you for being a CGR member. We appreciate your business. Enjoy your new and improved card!
