Congratulations! Your enhanced CGR Credit Union VISA® ATM/debit card with chip technology is on the way. All members who have been using a CGR Mastercard will receive a new VISA® card in the mail within the next 7-10 business days. If you do not receive a card by April 26, 2019, please call 478-745-0494 to notify us.

 

What to do when you receive your new card:

  • Activate your new card by calling the number on the sticker attached to the front of your new card.
  • Destroy your old Mastercard debit card. All Mastercard activity will be shut off effective April 29, 2019. You will need to destroy your Mastercard and activate your new VISA® card by this date.
  • Update merchants who automatically bill you and/or with whom you have your debit card on file for charges (Utilities, Cable provider, Amazon, Netflix, etc).

You can access additional information online at www.cgrcu.org/DebitCard2019.pdf. If you have any questions, please call us. We are here for you and want to help make this conversion as pain-free as possible.

 

Thank you for being a CGR member. We appreciate your business. Enjoy your new and improved card!
